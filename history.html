The History of France

Discover the rich, fascinating history of France through its significant moments and iconic figures.

The French Revolution

The French Revolution (1789-1799) was a period of social and political upheaval in France that drastically changed the country and the world.

Napoleon's Empire

Napoleon Bonaparte rose to prominence during the French Revolution and became the Emperor of France, leading his armies to victory in numerous battles.

World War I

France was a major player in World War I (1914-1918), experiencing devastating losses but also emerging victorious in the battlefields of Europe.

World War II & D-Day

The Normandy landings (D-Day) in 1944 marked a significant turning point in World War II and led to the liberation of France from Nazi occupation.
