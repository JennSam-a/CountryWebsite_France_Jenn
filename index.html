French Cuisine

France is famous for its food and wine. Enjoy croissants, baguettes, escargot, and a variety of cheeses. Every region has its own specialties that will delight your taste buds.

Fun Facts About France

  • France is the most visited country in the world.
  • The Eiffel Tower was completed in 1889 and is over 300 meters tall.
  • French is spoken not only in France but in many countries worldwide.
  • France produces over 400 types of cheese!
  • The Louvre Museum in Paris is the largest art museum in the world.
